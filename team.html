ESK Design is a trio of designers who met while studying Digital Design and Development at BCIT.
We worked so well together in school, so we thought why not keep it going!
ESK Design = Emma Shae Keith Design


Emma Schram

Fully caffeinated 24/7, Emma is enthusiastic, energetic and enjoys a challenge.
She takes care of all the writing you see at ESK, and CSS is her best friend.
When not working at ESK, Emma can be found in a cafe enjoying a good cup of coffee or outside photographing the world.


Shae Scotten

Shae loves everything design, and isn't afraid to tackle it head first.
His passion is graphics and music but loves to do everything in between,
and can't be torn away from some good coding. When Shae isn't working
he spends his time writing music and watching the latest movies.


Keith Chen

Keith is a perfectionist and aspiring artist, with an affection for everything digital.
His attention to detail and love for visual arts has lead him to ESK.
Outside of work, Keith likes to express his competitive nature by crushing nerds in online video games.
